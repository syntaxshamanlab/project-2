He is constantly prepared for "the thing that nobody thinks will happen," even when sober, acknowledging this as a "huge burden to carry" and a component of his **PTSD**. This strategic vigilance allows him "not to react emotionally if the unthinkable occurs".
*   **Self-Sacrifice and Fortitude:** Ryan's past as a father figure (to Jax) and provider instilled in him the ability to "put my needs aside and even if they are a big deal to me, I can just suck it up". This trait is evident in his willingness to endure discomfort (like a freezing truck for his family) and manage stressful situations for others.
*   **Political Views:** He shifted from being a Democrat (because he was poor) to believing the Democrat way of governance is "destructive" for American people, particularly for men without purpose, stating, "What's the best way to destroy a person? Give them free money". He finds purpose by making videos.
